MARS THE RED PLANET

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is the second smallest planet in the solar system
Remarkably that the gender symbol for male is astronomical and astrological symbol of the planet Mars. Mars is named after the Roman god of war. In different cultures, Mars represents masculinity and youth.

INTERESTING FACTS

  • Mars and Earth have approximately the same landmass
  • Mars is home to the tallest mountain in the solar system
  • Only 18 missions to Mars have been successful
  • Mars has the largest dust storms in the solar system
  • On Mars the Sun appears about half the size as it does on Earth
  • Pieces of Mars have fallen to Earth
  • Mars takes its name from the Roman god of war
  • There are signs of liquid water on Mars

MARS PROFILE

  • Equatorial radius: 3,396 km
  • Polar radius: 3,376 km
  • Surface area: 144,798,500 km2
  • Volume: 1.6318*1011 km3
  • Mass: 6.4171*10^23 kg
  • Moons: 2 (Phobos & Deimos)
  • Orbit Distance: 227,943,824 km
  • Orbit Period: 687 days (1.9 years)
  • Surface Temperature: -153 to 20 ℃
